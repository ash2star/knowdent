The teeth are the hardest substances in the human body. Besides being essential for chewing, the teeth play an important role in speech. Parts of the teeth include:

  • Enamel:It is the hardest outer covering of the tooth and also the most highly mineralized substance in our body as it is mostly made of calcium.
  • Gingiva(gums):The gums are made up of tissues know as the mucosal tissue. Gums can effect our overall health.
